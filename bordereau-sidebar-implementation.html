📋 What is a Bordereau?

Definition: A bordereau is a unique shipment or delivery slip number that ties a physical consignment back to its digital order record.

For Fofana Stock Tracker:

  • • Tracking: Links physical packages to digital orders
  • • Logistics: Helps warehouse staff identify and process shipments
  • • Customer Service: Provides reference number for customer inquiries
  • • Audit Trail: Creates traceable connection between order and delivery
  • • Inventory Management: Ensures accurate stock movement tracking

Format: 12-digit unique number (e.g., 172345678912) automatically generated for each order
